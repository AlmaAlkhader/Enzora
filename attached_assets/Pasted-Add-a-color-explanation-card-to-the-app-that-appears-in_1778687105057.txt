Add a color explanation card to the app 
that appears in the right places to help 
elderly patients understand what each 
color means.

---

### 1. Color meaning card — show on Home screen

Below the hero status card on the home 
screen, add a small "What do the colors 
mean?" section.

Title: "What the colors mean 🎨"
Arabic: "ماذا تعني الألوان 🎨"

Show 3 simple rows, one per color:

Row 1:
🟡 Big yellow circle
Bold text: "Yellow — Everything is fine"
Small text: "Your wound is healing normally. 
Keep it clean and dry."
Arabic:
"أصفر — كل شيء على ما يرام"
"جرحك يلتئم بشكل طبيعي. 
 حافظ عليه نظيفاً وجافاً."

Row 2:
🟢 Big green circle  
Bold text: "Green — Worth keeping an eye on"
Small text: "The sensor noticed a small change. 
Nothing urgent, but please watch your wound 
and consider calling your doctor."
Arabic:
"أخضر — يستحق المتابعة"
"لاحظ الجهاز تغيراً بسيطاً. 
 لا شيء عاجلاً، لكن راقب جرحك 
 وفكر في الاتصال بطبيبك."

Row 3:
🔵 Big blue circle
Bold text: "Blue — Please call your doctor"
Small text: "The sensor detected a sign of 
infection. Please contact your doctor 
as soon as possible."
Arabic:
"أزرق — يرجى الاتصال بطبيبك"
"اكتشف الجهاز علامة عدوى.
 يرجى التواصل مع طبيبك 
 في أقرب وقت ممكن."

Card style:
- White background, border-radius 16px
- Soft shadow
- Each row: colored circle (24px) on the 
  left, text on the right
- Divider line between rows
- Collapsed by default — show only the 
  title with a ▼ arrow
- Tapping the title expands/collapses it
- This way it does not take space unless 
  the patient wants to read it

### 2. Show color explanation when status changes

When the status changes to green or blue,
show a friendly explanation banner 
ABOVE the hero status card:

When GREEN:
Soft orange banner with 🟢 icon:
English:
"The sensor detected a color change 
 in your bandage. This could be an 
 early sign. Nothing to panic about — 
 just keep an eye on it and contact 
 your doctor if you are worried."

Arabic:
"اكتشف الجهاز تغيراً في لون الضمادة.
 قد يكون هذا علامة مبكرة. 
 لا داعي للقلق — فقط راقب جرحك 
 وتواصل مع طبيبك إذا كنت قلقاً."

When BLUE:
Soft red banner with 🔵 icon:
English:
"The sensor is showing a blue reading 
 which may mean an infection has started. 
 Please call your doctor today. 
 If you feel unwell, go to the hospital."

Arabic:
"يُظهر الجهاز قراءة زرقاء قد تعني 
 بدء عدوى. يرجى الاتصال بطبيبك اليوم.
 إذا كنت تشعر بتوعك، اذهب إلى المستشفى."

Banner style:
- Rounded card, not a harsh alert box
- Large readable text, minimum 16px
- Dismiss button (X) in top right corner
- Reappears every time status changes 
  (not permanently dismissed)

### 3. Add color guide to onboarding slide 2

On onboarding slide 2 "Real-Time Color 
Detection", replace the current color 
scale with 3 clear labeled items:

🟡 Yellow = Normal
🟢 Green = Watch closely  
🔵 Blue = Call your doctor

Each with a one-line description.
Large font, simple language.
No technical terms.

---

### 4. Color guide accessible from profile

In the Profile screen add a help section:
Button: "Color Guide 🎨"
Arabic: "دليل الألوان 🎨"
Tapping opens a full screen with all 
three color explanations written in 
large simple text — like a reference 
card the patient can always come back to.

This is especially useful if an elderly 
patient forgets what a color means at 
2am when they get an alert.
