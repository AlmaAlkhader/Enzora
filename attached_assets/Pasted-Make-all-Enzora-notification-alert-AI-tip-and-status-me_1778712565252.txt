Make all Enzora notification, alert, AI tip, and status messages shorter, warmer, and less boring.

Problem:
The current messages are too long, repetitive, and feel boring. Elderly users need short, clear, friendly messages that tell them exactly what to do.

Do not change authentication.
Do not change Firebase ESP32 sensor logic.
Do not redesign unrelated UI.
Only improve message wording and message length.

General message style:
- Short
- Warm
- Human
- Reassuring
- Clear
- Not robotic
- Not too medical
- Not too dramatic unless urgent
- One clear action only
- No long paragraphs
- No repeated explanations
- No technical terms
- No raw RGB values

Message length rules:
- Push notifications: maximum 1 short sentence in body.
- Home status message: maximum 2 short lines.
- Alert card message: maximum 1–2 short lines.
- Daily AI care tip: maximum 3 short sentences.
- WhatsApp/share message: maximum 4 short lines.
- Avoid long blocks of text.

DAILY AI CARE TIP

Make daily AI tips shorter and more natural.

System instruction update:
“You are Enzora Care Assistant. Write short, warm wound-care tips. Do not diagnose. Do not scare the patient. Use simple words. Maximum 3 short sentences. Give one helpful tip and one clear action.”

Rules:
- Maximum 3 short sentences.
- No long introductions.
- No repeated “you are doing great” every time.
- Use one gentle motivational phrase only.
- Include one practical tip.
- Include one action for today.
- If blue, be urgent but brief.

Examples:

Yellow:
“Good morning, Sarah. Your wound looks stable today. Keep it clean and dry.”

Arabic:
“صباح الخير يا سارة. يبدو الجرح مستقراً اليوم. حافظي عليه نظيفاً وجافاً.”

Green:
“Good morning, Sarah. A small change was noticed. Check the wound today and call your doctor if pain or swelling increases.”

Arabic:
“صباح الخير يا سارة. تم ملاحظة تغير بسيط. افحصي الجرح اليوم واتصلي بالطبيب إذا زاد الألم أو التورم.”

Blue:
“Good morning, Sarah. Enzora detected an alert reading. Call your doctor today.”

Arabic:
“صباح الخير يا سارة. اكتشف إنزورا قراءة تنبيه. اتصلي بطبيبك اليوم.”
