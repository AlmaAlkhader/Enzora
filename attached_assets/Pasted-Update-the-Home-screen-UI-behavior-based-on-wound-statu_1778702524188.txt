Update the Home screen UI behavior based on wound status.

Important:
The Home screen should not look the same for all statuses.

Current problem:
When the wound status is infected / blue, the Home screen still looks too calm and friendly. This is dangerous because the patient may not understand that they need to act quickly.

Task:
Make the Home screen visually change depending on the wound status.

Status behavior:

1. Yellow / Normal status
The screen should feel calm and reassuring.
Use:
- soft yellow accent
- friendly status icon
- title: “Wound is Normal”
- message: “Your wound shows no signs of infection. Keep it clean and dry.”
- primary action: “View Details”

2. Green / Watch closely status
The screen should feel cautious but not scary.
Use:
- soft green or warm warning accent
- title: “Watch Closely”
- message: “The sensor noticed a small change. Keep an eye on your wound and contact your doctor if you are worried.”
- primary action: “View Details”
- secondary action: “Call Doctor” if doctor phone exists

3. Blue / Infected status
The screen should look clearly urgent.
This is the most important change.

When status is blue / infected:
- Change the main hero status card to an urgent style.
- Use a soft red / alert background or red-tinted card.
- Use red accent color #EF233C for borders, icons, and key labels.
- Add a clear urgent icon.
- Make the title larger and stronger:
  “Infection Detected”
- Arabic:
  “تم اكتشاف عدوى”
- Use clear text:
  “Please call your doctor today. If you feel unwell, go to the hospital.”
- Arabic:
  “يرجى الاتصال بطبيبك اليوم. إذا كنت تشعر بتوعك، اذهب إلى المستشفى.”

Urgent action buttons for infected status:
Show these buttons directly inside or below the hero card:

Primary red button:
English: “Call Doctor Now”
Arabic: “اتصل بالطبيب الآن”

If no doctor phone exists, show:
English: “Add Doctor Contact”
Arabic: “أضف رقم الطبيب”

Secondary button:
English: “View Details”
Arabic: “عرض التفاصيل”

If emergency contact exists, also show:
English: “Call Emergency Contact”
Arabic: “اتصل بجهة الطوارئ”

Design requirements for infected status:
- The infected state must be visually different from normal.
- Do not make it overly terrifying, but it must be impossible to miss.
- Use a red-tinted alert card, stronger border, and larger action button.
- The patient should immediately understand: “I need to call someone.”
- Do not hide the call action lower down the page.
- Do not require the patient to open Wound Detail to understand the risk.

Recommended infected layout:
- Header
- Device connected pill
- Large urgent hero card:
  - urgent icon
  - “Infection Detected”
  - short explanation
  - last check time
  - “Call Doctor Now” button
  - “View Details” secondary button
- Then show only essential supporting info.
- Reduce less important sections when infected.

When status is infected:
Hide or move down non-essential content:
- care tips should be secondary
- color guide should be secondary
- stats should be minimized
- the urgent action card should dominate the screen

Also update Alerts behavior if needed:
- When status becomes blue, create/show an infection alert.
- Alert should link to the infected wound detail page if possible.

Do not change:
- authentication
- database architecture
- Firebase sensor connection
- ESP32 logic
- unrelated screens

Final goal:
The Home screen should feel calm when the wound is normal, cautious when early signs appear, and clearly urgent/action-focused when infection is detected.
